About Us

A Bus Management System is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline the operations of bus transportation companies. It typically includes features for: Route and Schedule Management: Plan and manage bus routes and schedules efficiently. Ticketing: Facilitate both online and offline ticket sales, making it easier for passengers to book tickets. Fleet Management: Monitor and maintain the bus fleet, including tracking maintenance schedules and vehicle performance. Driver Management: Manage driver schedules, assignments, and performance.
